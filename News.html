Zak Lee-Green announces his retirement from rowing

After an outstanding decade of representing Great Britain, World Silver Medalist Zak Lee-Green, announces his retirement. "The whole experience was a lot of fun. It was always on wheels and just kept going. I'll look back on that as one of my favourites.
He says he knew the time was right for him to step away from the squad: "I had a bit of a cry in the car on the way home, but then knew I'd made the right decision when I felt good the next day." "I've had a great time, going to amazing places and competing alongside the best rowing athletes in the world".
